Who Is Alper Bekat?

Social media content moderator/creator

I was born in 1993 in İzmir. I started my music career at the age of six by taking bağlama training. After three years of bağlama training, I started flute education in primary school with the guidance of my music teacher. While studying classical western music for many years, I became interested in rock music and started playing the guitar. Guitar, which I started to learn on my own, became my profession after many years and I started to work as a guitarist and lead vocal in bands since my university years.Later, my brother and I continued our stage work for years with the Migren band we founded. At the same time, I focused on theater and acting education during my university years. After a few years of education, we started to play plays by establishing our own theater in İzmir Alsancak with my fellow actors. My music and theater life, which continued until 2020, came to an end with the pandemic. After the pandemic period ended, I moved to Germany with a sudden decision and found a job in the social media sector. In October 2022, I started uploading videos on Youtube explaining my process of moving to Germany and I continue to help people who are looking for guidance.
